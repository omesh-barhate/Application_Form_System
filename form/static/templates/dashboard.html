Bachelor of Management Studies (B.M.S.) Program Overview

The B.M.S. program is a 3-year undergraduate degree affiliated with the University of Mumbai, offering comprehensive knowledge in management principles and practices.

Renowned industry leaders are invited as guest lecturers, providing valuable insights and sharing their experiences with our students, bridging the gap between academia and the corporate world.

Our faculty members are accomplished academics actively involved in research or possess extensive industry experience. This ensures a unique blend of emerging ideas and practical knowledge for our students.
